Create a world-class, ultra-premium homepage design for a modern medical university. The design must feel more refined and elegant than top global institutions like Harvard Medical School, Stanford Medicine, and Johns Hopkins.

IMPORTANT:
Do NOT follow traditional or outdated university website layouts. Avoid grid-heavy, cluttered, or government-style designs. Focus on a clean, storytelling-driven, luxury digital experience.

BRAND IDENTITY:

* Primary Color: Deep Emerald Green (#0F5D3F)
* Secondary: Dark Green (#0B3D2E)
* Accent: Premium Gold/Yellow (#F4B400)
* Background: Soft neutral (#F8FAFC)
* Text: Charcoal (#1A1A1A)

STYLE:

* Minimal, elegant, high-end
* Strong use of whitespace
* Soft shadows and depth
* Rounded corners (12–16px)
* Smooth animations and transitions
* Editorial + SaaS hybrid design

TYPOGRAPHY:

* Headings: Poppins or Playfair Display (bold and elegant)
* Body: Inter (clean and readable)

HOMEPAGE EXPERIENCE FLOW:

1. IMMERSIVE HERO SECTION

* Full screen height
* Cinematic background (medical campus or students)
* Dark green gradient overlay
* Main headline:
  "Redefining the Future of Medical Education"
* Short subheading
* CTA buttons:

  * Apply Now (gold button)
  * Explore Programs (outline)
* Centered and spacious layout

2. QUICK ACCESS (MINIMAL)

* 3–4 premium cards (not heavy grid)
* Admissions, Results, Downloads, Notices
* Glass-style cards with soft shadow
* Hover lift animation

3. VICE CHANCELLOR MESSAGE (ELEGANT)

* Two-column layout
* Left: portrait image with rounded corners and soft shadow
* Right:

  * Small label: "Message from the Vice Chancellor"
  * Name and designation (bold)
  * Short refined excerpt (3–4 lines only)
  * "Read Full Message" button
* Keep it minimal, professional, and prestigious

4. DISCOVER EXPERIENCE (INSTEAD OF BASIC GRIDS)

* Large interactive panels:

  * Study Medicine
  * Clinical Training
  * Research Innovation
* Each panel visually rich with image + overlay
* Hover animations (expand or highlight)

5. CAMPUS & FACILITIES (VISUAL STORY)

* Edge-to-edge images
* Clean layout
* Minimal text
* Parallax or smooth transitions

6. NEWS & INSIGHTS (EDITORIAL STYLE)

* One featured large story
* 2–3 smaller stories
* Clean typography and spacing
* No clutter

7. IMPACT / STATS SECTION

* Dark green background
* Gold-accent numbers
* Animated counters
* Large typography for impact

8. SOCIAL / FACEBOOK FEED

* Clean embedded feed section
* Balanced spacing
* Not cluttered

9. FINAL CALL TO ACTION

* Strong minimal section
* Heading:
  "Begin Your Journey in Medicine"
* Prominent gold button

UX PRINCIPLES:

* Clear visual hierarchy
* Strong call-to-actions
* Mobile-first responsive design
* Smooth scrolling and subtle animations
* Content breathing space

STRICTLY AVOID:

* Old-style WordPress layouts
* Heavy grid systems
* Long paragraphs
* Cluttered sections
* Boxy designs

OUTPUT:
Generate a visually stunning, modern, luxurious homepage design that feels like a flagship global institution website. The layout should be clean, structured, and ready to implement in Elementor or similar builders.
